Dice Rules

üé≤ BASIC MOVEMENT

Standard 2-dice system (6-sided each). Move forward equal to total rolled. Actions triggered based on landing tile.

‚ö° DOUBLES RULE

Roll doubles ‚Üí One extra roll. Roll 3 consecutive doubles ‚Üí Sent directly to Detention. Turn ends immediately.

üîÑ EXTRA TURN CAP

Max 2 turns in a row. Extra turn granted by doubles OR if a Special Tile explicitly grants it. No stacking beyond 2.
